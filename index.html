About Me

I also love learning new things and have been excited to expand my skills into tech! I firmly believe good design can have a positive impact on a brand's image. Ask me how I can help your brand stand out!

I also enjoy baking and movies. I love making chocolate chip cookies, and my favorite movie is either Dr. Strangelove or Patton.
